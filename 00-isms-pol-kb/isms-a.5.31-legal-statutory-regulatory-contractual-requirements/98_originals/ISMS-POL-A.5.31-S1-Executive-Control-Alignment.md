## SECTION 3: Scope & Applicability

### WRITING INSTRUCTIONS:
Define the scope of this framework clearly:

1. **What This Framework Covers**:
   - ALL potentially applicable regulations (legal, statutory, regulatory, contractual)
   - Identification and assessment processes
   - Requirements extraction and mapping
   - Change management and evidence framework
   - Applies across ALL of [Organization]'s operations and jurisdictions

2. **Framework Applicability**:
   - This framework is **universal and industry-agnostic**
   - [Organization] uses framework to identify THEIR applicable regulations
   - No specific regulations assumed in the framework itself
   - Scalable to any regulatory landscape

3. **Relationship to ISMS-POL-00**:
   - CRITICAL SECTION
   - Explain that ISMS-POL-00 is the **regulatory register** (the "what")
   - This framework (A.5.31) defines **HOW POL-00 is maintained** (the "how")
   - Clear governance relationship
   - POL-00 is updated using processes defined in this framework

4. **Out of Scope**:
   - This framework does NOT provide legal advice
   - Does NOT interpret specific regulations
   - Does NOT replace qualified legal counsel
   - Provides systematic methodology, not legal opinions

    - Relationship diagram

## SECTION 4: Roles & Responsibilities

### WRITING INSTRUCTIONS:
Define clear accountability for regulatory compliance framework:

### KEY ROLES:
1. **Compliance Officer / Legal Function**
   - Identifies potentially applicable regulations
   - Performs applicability assessments
   - Monitors regulatory landscape for changes
   - Conducts legal interpretation (or coordinates with external counsel)
   - Approves applicability determinations
   - Reviews requirements extraction for accuracy

2. **ISMS Manager**
   - Owns the regulatory compliance framework (this document)
   - Coordinates control mapping activities
   - Maintains control mapping matrices
   - Tracks gaps and remediation
   - Reports compliance status to management
   - Coordinates framework updates

3. **Control Owners**
   - Implement controls addressing regulatory requirements
   - Provide evidence of control implementation
   - Report control effectiveness
   - Support mapping validation

4. **Internal Audit / Compliance Team**
   - Validates evidence completeness
   - Performs internal compliance audits
   - Verifies framework effectiveness
   - Identifies improvement opportunities

5. **Executive Management**
   - Approves significant compliance decisions
   - Allocates resources for compliance activities
   - Receives compliance status reports
   - Approves risk acceptance for gaps

### STRUCTURE:
Use a table format:
```
| Role | Responsibilities | Authority | Accountability |
```

- Extract requirements: R=Legal/Compliance, A=ISMS Mgr, C=ISMS Mgr

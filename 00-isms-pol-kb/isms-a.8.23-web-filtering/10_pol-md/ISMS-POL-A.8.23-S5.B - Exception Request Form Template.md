## B.1 Purpose and Scope

This annex provides a **standardized exception request form** to implement the exception management process defined in policy section S2.4.

**Purpose**:
- Ensure consistent information capture for all exception requests
- Facilitate risk assessment and approval decisions
- Create audit trail for exceptions
- Support exception lifecycle management

**Usage**: All requests for exceptions to web filtering policies must use this form (or equivalent fields in ticketing/workflow systems)

**Audience**: End Users, Business Unit Managers, Security Team

## B.3 Submission Instructions

### B.3.1 How to Submit

**Option 1 - Self-Service Portal** (preferred):
- Access exception request portal at: [URL]
- Complete form electronically
- Submit for Security Team review
- Track status online

**Option 2 - Email Submission**:
- Complete this form (PDF or Word format)
- Email to: security-team@organization.com
- Subject: "Web Filtering Exception Request - [Your Name]"
- Expect acknowledgment within 1 business day

**Option 3 - IT Service Desk Ticket**:
- Open ticket via IT Service Desk portal
- Select category: "Security → Web Filtering Exception"
- Complete form fields in ticket
- Security Team will be automatically notified

### B.3.2 Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

**Acknowledgment**: Within 1 business day  
**Review Completion**:
- Standard requests: 3-5 business days
- Urgent requests: 1 business day (requires manager approval for urgency)
- Emergency requests: 4 business hours (see Emergency Process)

**Implementation**: Within 2 business days of approval

### B.3.3 Emergency Exception Process

**When to use**: Production outage, critical deadline, security incident investigation

**Process**:
1. Contact Security Team directly (phone/chat) - do not wait for ticket
2. Explain urgency and business impact
3. Obtain verbal approval from Security Team Lead (LOW/MEDIUM risk) or CISO (HIGH risk)
4. Submit formal request within 1 business day (retroactive documentation)
5. Formal approval completed within 3 business days

**Abuse of emergency process**: Repeated emergencies from same requestor will be escalated to management

## B.4 Exception Tracking

All approved exceptions are recorded in the **Exception Register** maintained by Security Team.

**Exception Register contains**:
- Exception ID (unique identifier)
- Requestor and affected users
- Exception type and details
- Business justification
- Risk rating and compensating controls
- Approval authority and date
- Implementation date
- Expiration/review date
- Current status (Active, Expired, Revoked, Under Review)

**Reporting**: Security Team generates monthly exception reports for CISO review

---

## B.5 Exception Renewal

Temporary exceptions **DO NOT** auto-renew. Users must request renewal:

**Renewal Process**:
1. Security Team sends expiration warning (30 days, 7 days, 1 day before expiration)
2. User submits renewal request using same form
3. Re-confirm business need (justification may have changed)
4. Security Team reassesses risk (same approval authority as original)
5. If not renewed, exception automatically revokes on expiration date

---

## B.6 Exception Revocation

Exceptions may be revoked:
- At expiration (if not renewed)
- At requestor's request
- Due to security incident (emergency revocation)
- Due to policy change (exception no longer needed)
- Due to non-use (if exception granted but never used)

**Revocation notification**: Users notified at least 24 hours in advance (except emergency revocations)

## B.7 Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: How long does approval take?**  
A: Standard requests: 3-5 business days. Urgent: 1 business day. Emergency: 4 hours.

**Q: Can I request an exception on behalf of my team?**  
A: Yes, if you are a manager or have delegation authority. Specify all affected users.

**Q: What if my request is denied?**  
A: Security Team will explain reason and suggest alternatives. You may appeal to CISO with additional justification.

**Q: How often do I need to renew?**  
A: Temporary exceptions: before expiration date. Permanent exceptions: reviewed annually/semi-annually/quarterly based on risk.

**Q: Can I expedite my request?**  
A: Mark as "urgent" and have your manager approve urgency. Provide justification for expedited handling.

**Q: What if the site I need is incorrectly blocked (false positive)?**  
A: Report via false positive process (faster than exception request). Security Team will recategorize if legitimate.

**Q: Do I need approval from my manager?**  
A: Not required for submission, but Security Team may contact your manager during review (especially for MEDIUM/HIGH risk).

## Purpose

This document provides standardized forms and templates to support operational implementation of ISMS-POL-A.8.10 (Information Deletion Policy).

**Forms Included**:
1. Data Subject Erasure Request Form (GDPR Article 17 / Swiss nDSG)
2. Deletion Verification Checklist
3. Policy Exception Request Form
4. Deletion Log Template
5. Certificate of Destruction Template
6. Legal Hold Notification Template
7. Third-Party Deletion Request Template

**Usage**: These forms may be updated based on operational experience without requiring policy revision. Forms are working documents adapted to organisational needs.

---

### Instructions

- Maintain completed forms in evidence register for 3+ years

## Executive Summary

This policy establishes [Organisation]'s requirements when acting as PII Processor to provide customers with the means to comply with their obligations to PII principals — in accordance with ISO/IEC 27701:2025 Control A.2.3.2.

**Scope**: All PII processing activities conducted by [Organisation] as PII Processor; all mechanisms and capabilities that enable customer controllers to fulfil their data subject rights obligations.

**Role Applicability**: This policy applies to [Organisation] acting as **PII Processor only**. Control A.2.3.2 is processor-specific (Table A.2).

---

# Scope and Applicability

## ISO/IEC 27701:2025 Control Statement

**Control A.2.3.2 — Comply with obligations to PII principals**
> *The organization shall provide the customer with the means to comply with its obligations related to PII principals.*

## Regulatory Framework

**Tier 1: Mandatory Compliance** (per PRIV-POL-00):

- **EU GDPR**: Article 28(3)(e) (processor shall assist the controller with responding to requests to exercise data subjects' rights); Article 28(3)(f) (assist with security, breach notification, DPIA obligations)
- **CH FADP**: Article 9(2) (processor shall only process as contractually permitted and take measures ensuring data protection)
- **ISO/IEC 27701:2025**: Control A.2.3.2 (normative)

# Policy Statements

## Obligation to Provide Means for Customer Compliance

When [Organisation] processes PII on behalf of a customer controller, [Organisation] SHALL provide the customer with the means to comply with the customer's obligations to data subjects (PII principals). This obligation operates regardless of whether the customer explicitly requests specific assistance — [Organisation] has a proactive duty to ensure the means are available.

### Data Subject Rights Fulfilment Capabilities

[Organisation] SHALL make available to customers the technical and operational capabilities necessary to:

**Access and portability**: Retrieve all PII held for a specific data subject in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format upon customer instruction.

**Rectification**: Update or correct PII for a specific data subject upon customer instruction.

**Erasure**: Delete all PII for a specific data subject across all systems where [Organisation] processes that customer's PII, upon customer instruction — including backups, within the timeframe defined in the processor agreement.

**Restriction of processing**: Mark specific data subjects' PII as restricted and ensure no further processing occurs (storage only) upon customer instruction.

**Objection**: Cease processing specific data subjects' PII for specified purposes upon customer instruction.

### Capability Availability

The means to fulfil the above SHALL be:

- Available within the timeframe required for the customer to meet their regulatory obligations (typically within the applicable response window less reasonable lead time)
- Documented in PRIV-IMP-A.2.3.2-TG, specifying how each capability is accessed by the customer
- Maintained as a contractual commitment in processor agreements (per PRIV-POL-A.2.2.2-7)

### Notification to Customers

[Organisation] SHALL forward to the customer, without undue delay, any data subject rights requests received directly by [Organisation] (e.g., a data subject contacting [Organisation] directly). [Organisation] shall NOT respond directly to data subject rights requests without explicit customer authorisation, as the customer is the controller.

# Roles and Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibilities |
|------|-----------------|
| **Data Protection Officer (DPO)** | Ensures rights fulfilment capabilities are available; reviews contractual commitments; handles escalations from customers regarding data subject requests |
| **IT Security Team** | Implements technical capabilities (data extraction, deletion, restriction flags) for data subject rights fulfilment |
| **Customer Success** | Coordinates customer requests for data subject rights fulfilment; tracks requests through to completion |

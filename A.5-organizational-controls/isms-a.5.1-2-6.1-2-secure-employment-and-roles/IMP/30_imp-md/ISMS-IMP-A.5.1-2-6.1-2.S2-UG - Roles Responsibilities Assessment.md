## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Assessment Objective

This assessment verifies [Organization]'s compliance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Control A.5.2 (Information Security Roles and Responsibilities) as implemented through ISMS-POL-A.5.1-2-6.1-2, Section 5.

**Control A.5.2 Requirement:**
> *Information security roles and responsibilities should be defined and allocated according to the organisation needs.*

**Assessment Focus:**
- Security role definitions (CISO, DPO, Security Team, System Owners, Users)
- RACI matrix completeness and accuracy
- Accountability assignment clarity
- Role documentation quality
- Training and awareness for role holders
- Role-based access alignment

### 1.2 Relationship to Parent Policy

This assessment implements verification procedures for **ISMS-POL-A.5.1-2-6.1-2, Section 5: Roles & Responsibilities Requirements**.

**Policy Requirements Being Assessed:**
- **Section 5.1**: Organizational Security Structure (CISO, DPO, Security Committee)
- **Section 5.2**: Key Security Roles (defined roles with clear responsibilities)
- **Section 5.3**: RACI Framework (Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed)
- **Section 5.4**: Role Documentation (job descriptions, role definitions, delegation)
- **Section 5.5**: Training and Awareness (role-specific security training)
- **Section 5.6**: Role Review and Update (annual review, change management)

**Evidence Collected**: Role definitions, job descriptions, RACI matrices, organizational charts, training records, role assignments

### 1.3 Assessment Frequency

**Standard Schedule:**
- **Quarterly**: All organizations (routine compliance verification)
- **Annual**: Comprehensive role review and RACI validation
- **Triggered**: After organizational restructuring, leadership changes, or audit findings

**Why Quarterly?**
- Organizational changes happen continuously (new hires, promotions, departures)
- Quarterly assessment catches role assignment gaps early
- Ensures role clarity maintained during growth phases
- Supports audit readiness

### 1.4 Assessment Ownership

**Primary Owner**: CISO or HR Manager (joint ownership recommended)

**Participants**:
- CISO (for security role verification)
- HR Manager (for job description alignment)
- Department Heads (for role assignment verification)
- Data Protection Officer (for DPO role verification)
- Training Manager (for training record verification)
- Internal Audit (for independent verification)

**Time Investment**: 3-5 hours for initial assessment, 2-3 hours for quarterly updates

## 2. Assessment Methodology

### 2.1 Assessment Approach

This assessment uses a **role-based verification approach**:

**Step 1: Role Discovery**
- Identify all security-relevant roles in organization
- Catalog roles by category (Leadership, Technical, Operational, User)
- Document role metadata (title, department, current holder)

**Step 2: Role Definition Verification**
- Verify each role has documented definition
- Check responsibilities are clear and specific
- Confirm accountability assignments
- Validate role documentation accessibility

**Step 3: RACI Matrix Assessment**
- Verify RACI matrix exists for key security processes
- Check RACI completeness (all roles assigned)
- Confirm RACI clarity (no ambiguous assignments)
- Validate RACI accuracy (assignments match actual practice)

**Step 4: Role Assignment Verification**
- Confirm each role has assigned individual(s)
- Verify role holders are aware of responsibilities
- Check role-based access aligns with role definition
- Validate backup/deputy assignments

**Step 5: Training Assessment**
- Verify role-specific security training exists
- Check training completion rates
- Confirm training currency (refreshed annually)
- Validate training effectiveness

**Step 6: Gap Analysis**
- Identify roles without definitions (role coverage gaps)
- Identify RACI gaps (missing or unclear assignments)
- Identify unassigned roles (accountability gaps)
- Identify training gaps (untrained role holders)

### 2.2 Data Sources

**Primary Sources**:
- Organizational charts
- Job descriptions and role definitions
- RACI matrices (process documentation)
- Role assignment records (HR system, Active Directory)
- Training system (LMS, training records)
- Access control system (role-based permissions)

**Supporting Sources**:
- ISMS Master Policy (governance structure)
- Policy ownership assignments (from .S1 assessment)
- Interview responses (role holder awareness)
- Performance review documentation
- Delegation records

### 2.3 Compliance Criteria

**Compliant Role** meets ALL criteria:
- ✅ Role has documented definition (job description or role charter)
- ✅ Role responsibilities are clear and specific
- ✅ Role has assigned individual (not vacant)
- ✅ Role holder is aware of security responsibilities
- ✅ Role has RACI assignments (for key processes)
- ✅ Role-based access aligns with role definition
- ✅ Role holder has completed role-specific security training (within 12 months)

**Partial Compliance** occurs when:
- ⚠️ Role definition exists but incomplete (missing key responsibilities)
- ⚠️ Role assigned but role holder unaware of some responsibilities
- ⚠️ RACI partially defined (some processes covered, others missing)
- ⚠️ Training completed but outdated (> 12 months)

**Non-Compliant** when:
- ❌ Role has no documented definition
- ❌ Role is vacant (unassigned)
- ❌ Role holder unaware of security responsibilities
- ❌ No RACI assignments (ambiguous accountability)
- ❌ Role-based access does not align with role definition
- ❌ No role-specific security training

### 2.4 Scoring Methodology

**Overall Compliance Score** = Weighted average across assessment domains

**Domain Weights**:
- Role Definition Completeness: 25% (roles must be defined)
- RACI Matrix Completeness: 25% (accountability must be clear)
- Role Assignment: 20% (roles must be filled)
- Role Documentation Quality: 15% (definitions must be accessible)
- Training Completion: 10% (role holders must be trained)
- Role-Based Access Alignment: 5% (technical controls must match roles)

**Compliance Score Interpretation**:
- **90-100%**: Excellent (audit-ready, no significant gaps)
- **80-89%**: Good (minor gaps, routine remediation)
- **70-79%**: Acceptable (moderate gaps, focused remediation needed)
- **60-69%**: Needs Improvement (significant gaps, priority remediation)
- **< 60%**: Critical (major gaps, immediate action required)

## 3. Security Role Taxonomy

### 3.1 Understanding the Role Hierarchy

**ISMS-POL-A.5.1-2-6.1-2 defines a four-tier role structure:**

**Tier 1: Security Leadership** (1-3 roles)
- Strategic governance and oversight
- Ultimate accountability for information security
- Examples: CISO, DPO, Security Committee Chair

**Tier 2: Security Management** (3-10 roles)
- Tactical security management and coordination
- Operational accountability for security domains
- Examples: Security Manager, IT Security Lead, Compliance Manager, Risk Manager

**Tier 3: Security Operational** (10-50 roles)
- Day-to-day security operations and implementation
- Responsible for executing security controls
- Examples: Security Analyst, System Administrator, Security Engineer, Incident Responder, Access Manager

**Tier 4: Security-Aware Roles** (All employees)
- General security awareness and compliance
- Responsible for following security policies
- Examples: All Employees, Contractors, Third-Party Users

**Assessment Scope**: This assessment focuses on **Tiers 1-3** (leadership, management, operational). Tier 4 (general users) covered in Security Awareness training assessment.

### 3.2 Minimum Required Security Roles

**Per ISO 27001:2022 and ISMS-POL-A.5.1-2-6.1-2:**

**Tier 1 - Security Leadership:**
- [ ] Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) or equivalent
- [ ] Data Protection Officer (DPO) - if required by regulation
- [ ] Information Security Committee/Board (governance body)

**Tier 2 - Security Management:**
- [ ] Security Manager (if CISO delegates operational management)
- [ ] IT Security Lead (technical security management)
- [ ] Compliance Manager (regulatory compliance oversight)
- [ ] Risk Manager (information security risk management)
- [ ] Business Continuity Manager (BC/DR coordination)

**Tier 3 - Security Operational:**
- [ ] Security Analyst (monitoring, alerts, analysis)
- [ ] Incident Response Coordinator (incident management)
- [ ] Access Control Administrator (identity & access management)
- [ ] Vulnerability Manager (vulnerability assessment & patching)
- [ ] Security Architect (security design & standards)
- [ ] System Administrators (with security responsibilities)
- [ ] Application Owners (application security)
- [ ] Data Owners (data classification & protection)

**Note**: Not all roles required for all organizations. Small organizations may combine roles. Large organizations may split roles further.

### 3.3 Role Discovery Process

**For Organizations Starting Fresh:**

**Step 1**: Review organizational chart
- Identify individuals with "security" in job title
- Identify IT roles with security responsibilities
- Identify business roles with data protection responsibilities

**Step 2**: Review job descriptions
- Extract security-related responsibilities
- Map responsibilities to security role taxonomy
- Identify gaps (security responsibilities not assigned)

**Step 3**: Interview department heads
- Ask: "Who handles security for your department?"
- Ask: "Who do you contact for security incidents?"
- Ask: "Who approves access to your systems?"

**Step 4**: Review ISMS policies
- Extract policy ownership assignments (from .S1 assessment)
- Identify roles referenced in policies
- Cross-check role existence vs. policy requirements

**For Organizations with Existing ISMS:**

**Step 1**: Export role inventory from ISMS documentation
- Role title, tier, current holder, department
- Last update date, next review date

**Step 2**: Validate role inventory completeness
- Cross-check against organizational chart
- Verify all security-related positions included
- Check for orphaned or duplicate roles

**Step 3**: Update role metadata
- Correct any outdated information
- Assign missing role holders
- Define review schedules for roles without them

### Sheet 2: Role_Inventory

**Purpose**: Master inventory of all security roles.

**Completion Process**:

**Step 1: Role Discovery**
- List ALL security-relevant roles in [Organization]
- Include Tier 1 (Leadership), Tier 2 (Management), Tier 3 (Operational)
- Exclude Tier 4 (general users) unless specific security user roles

**Step 2: Metadata Collection**
For each role, document:

**Q1: Role_ID**
- **Question**: What is the unique role identifier?
- **Format**: Organizational standard (e.g., ROLE-SEC-001, SR-CISO)
- **Example**: "SR-CISO", "SR-DPO", "SR-SECMGR-001"
- **Required**: YES
- **Tip**: Use consistent numbering scheme

**Q2: Role_Title**
- **Question**: What is the official role title?
- **Format**: Free text
- **Example**: "Chief Information Security Officer", "Security Analyst", "Data Protection Officer"
- **Required**: YES
- **Tip**: Use title from job description or org chart

**Q3: Role_Tier**
- **Question**: Which tier does this role belong to?
- **Dropdown**: Tier-1-Leadership / Tier-2-Management / Tier-3-Operational
- **Examples**:
  - Tier 1: "CISO", "DPO", "Security Committee Chair"
  - Tier 2: "Security Manager", "IT Security Lead", "Compliance Manager"
  - Tier 3: "Security Analyst", "Access Administrator", "Incident Responder"
- **Required**: YES

**Q4: Department**
- **Question**: Which department does this role belong to?
- **Format**: Free text
- **Examples**: "Information Security", "IT", "Compliance", "Risk Management", "HR"
- **Required**: YES

**Q5: Reports_To**
- **Question**: Who does this role report to?
- **Format**: Free text - Role title or Name
- **Examples**: "CISO", "CIO", "CEO", "Security Manager"
- **Required**: YES
- **Tip**: Establishes reporting hierarchy

**Q6: Current_Holder**
- **Question**: Who currently holds this role?
- **Format**: Free text - Name
- **Examples**: "Jane Smith", "John Doe", "[VACANT]"
- **Required**: YES
- **Note**: If vacant, enter "[VACANT]" to trigger vacancy tracking

**Q7: Employment_Type**
- **Question**: What is the employment type?
- **Dropdown**: Full-Time / Part-Time / Contractor / External-Consultant / Vacant
- **Required**: YES
- **Impact**: Affects role stability and training requirements

**Q8: Start_Date**
- **Question**: When did current holder start in this role?
- **Format**: Date (DD.MM.YYYY)
- **Required**: IF Current_Holder ≠ "[VACANT]"
- **Use**: Tenure tracking, onboarding verification

**Q9: Role_Definition_Exists**
- **Question**: Does a documented role definition exist?
- **Dropdown**: Yes / No
- **Definition**: Job description, role charter, or formal role documentation
- **Required**: YES
- **Validation**: If "No", triggers gap in Sheet 3

**Q10: Role_Definition_Location**
- **Question**: Where is the role definition documented?
- **Format**: Free text - URL or file path
- **Examples**:
  - "HR System: Job ID #12345"
  - "SharePoint: /HR/JobDescriptions/CISO.pdf"
  - "ISMS Documentation: /Roles/CISO-Charter.md"
- **Required**: IF Role_Definition_Exists = "Yes"

**Q11: Last_Review_Date**
- **Question**: When was this role definition last reviewed?
- **Format**: Date (DD.MM.YYYY)
- **Required**: IF Role_Definition_Exists = "Yes"
- **Note**: Roles should be reviewed annually

**Q12: Next_Review_Date**
- **Question**: When is the next role review scheduled?
- **Format**: Date (DD.MM.YYYY)
- **Required**: IF Role_Definition_Exists = "Yes"
- **Calculation**: Typically Last_Review_Date + 12 months
- **Status Trigger**: If Next_Review_Date < TODAY(), role definition is OVERDUE

**Q13: Key_Responsibilities_Count**
- **Question**: How many key security responsibilities does this role have?
- **Format**: Numeric (1-20)
- **Guidance**: Count distinct security responsibilities in role definition
- **Benchmark**: 
  - Tier 1: 5-10 strategic responsibilities
  - Tier 2: 8-15 tactical responsibilities
  - Tier 3: 5-12 operational responsibilities
- **Required**: IF Role_Definition_Exists = "Yes"

**Q14: RACI_Assigned**
- **Question**: Is this role assigned in RACI matrices?
- **Dropdown**: Yes / Partial / No
- **Definitions**:
  - Yes: Role has RACI assignments for all relevant processes
  - Partial: Role has some RACI assignments but not all
  - No: Role has no RACI assignments
- **Required**: YES
- **Validation**: If "No", triggers gap in Sheet 4

**Q15: Training_Required**
- **Question**: Is role-specific security training required?
- **Dropdown**: Yes / No
- **Guidance**:
  - Yes: Tier 1-3 roles typically require role-specific training
  - No: Generic roles may only need general security awareness
- **Required**: YES
- **Impact**: If "Yes", training completion tracked in Sheet 6

**Q16: Training_Completion_Status**
- **Question**: Has current role holder completed required training?
- **Dropdown**: Complete / Incomplete / Overdue / N/A
- **Definitions**:
  - Complete: Training completed within 12 months
  - Incomplete: Training not yet completed (role holder < 90 days tenure)
  - Overdue: Training not completed and role holder > 90 days tenure
  - N/A: Training_Required = "No" or Current_Holder = "[VACANT]"
- **Required**: IF Training_Required = "Yes"

**Q17: Role_Based_Access_Exists**
- **Question**: Does this role have defined access permissions?
- **Dropdown**: Yes / No / N/A
- **Guidance**:
  - Yes: Role has documented access permissions (AD groups, system permissions)
  - No: No documented access permissions
  - N/A: Role does not require system access
- **Required**: YES
- **Impact**: If "Yes", access alignment verified in Sheet 7

**Q18: Gap_Identified**
- **Question**: Does this role have any compliance gaps?
- **Dropdown**: No-Gap / Minor-Gap / Significant-Gap / Critical-Gap
- **Definitions**:
  - No-Gap: Fully compliant
  - Minor-Gap: Small issues, routine remediation
  - Significant-Gap: Moderate issues (e.g., role definition overdue, training overdue)
  - Critical-Gap: Major issues (e.g., role vacant, no definition, no RACI)
- **Required**: YES (populated during assessment)
- **Auto-Populate**: From Sheets 3-7 assessments

**Q19: Notes**
- **Question**: Any additional context?
- **Format**: Free text
- **Examples**:
  - "Role split from CISO role in Q4 2025"
  - "Recruiting for vacancy - expected hire Q2 2026"
  - "Part-time role (0.5 FTE) shared with IT Manager"
- **Required**: OPTIONAL

### Sheet 3: Role_Definition_Assessment

**Purpose**: Verify each role has documented, complete, and current definition.

**Assessment Focus**:
- Role definition existence
- Definition completeness (responsibilities, authorities, qualifications)
- Definition accessibility
- Definition currency (last review date)

**For each role in inventory:**

**Q1: Role_ID** (auto-populated from Sheet 2)

**Q2: Role_Title** (auto-populated from Sheet 2)

**Q3: Definition_Exists**
- **Question**: Does a documented role definition exist?
- **Auto-Populate**: From Sheet 2 (Role_Definition_Exists)
- **Compliant**: "Yes"

**Q4: Definition_Type**
- **Question**: What type of documentation defines this role?
- **Dropdown**: Job-Description / Role-Charter / Policy-Section / Org-Chart-Only / None
- **Definitions**:
  - Job-Description: Formal HR job description
  - Role-Charter: Dedicated role charter or responsibility matrix
  - Policy-Section: Defined in ISMS policy (e.g., CISO defined in Master Policy)
  - Org-Chart-Only: Listed in org chart but no detailed definition
  - None: No documentation
- **Required**: YES
- **Compliant**: "Job-Description", "Role-Charter", or "Policy-Section"

**Q5: Responsibilities_Documented**
- **Question**: Are key responsibilities documented?
- **Dropdown**: Complete / Partial / Missing
- **Verification**:
  - Complete: All key security responsibilities listed
  - Partial: Some responsibilities listed, but gaps identified
  - Missing: No responsibilities documented
- **Evidence**: Role definition document, job description
- **Compliant**: "Complete"

**Q6: Responsibilities_Specific**
- **Question**: Are responsibilities specific and actionable?
- **Dropdown**: Yes / Partial / No
- **Assessment**:
  - Yes: Responsibilities use action verbs, clear deliverables (e.g., "Conduct quarterly vulnerability scans and remediate critical findings within 30 days")
  - Partial: Some responsibilities vague (e.g., "Manage security")
  - No: All responsibilities vague or generic
- **Compliant**: "Yes"

**Q7: Authority_Documented**
- **Question**: Is role authority documented?
- **Dropdown**: Yes / Partial / No
- **Authority Elements**:
  - Decision-making authority (what can role approve/reject?)
  - Budget authority (spending limits)
  - Escalation authority (to whom and when?)
  - Delegation authority (can role delegate responsibilities?)
- **Compliant**: "Yes"

**Q8: Qualifications_Documented**
- **Question**: Are role qualifications documented?
- **Dropdown**: Yes / Partial / No
- **Qualifications Include**:
  - Education requirements (degree, certifications)
  - Experience requirements (years, domain expertise)
  - Skills requirements (technical, interpersonal)
- **Compliant**: "Yes" or "Partial"

**Q9: Definition_Accessibility**
- **Question**: Is role definition accessible to relevant stakeholders?
- **Dropdown**: Public / Internal / Restricted / Not-Accessible
- **Definitions**:
  - Public: Accessible to all (public job postings)
  - Internal: Accessible to all employees (intranet)
  - Restricted: Accessible to role holder + management only
  - Not-Accessible: No access (only HR has access)
- **Compliant**: "Public", "Internal", or "Restricted"

**Q10: Last_Review_Date**
- **Question**: When was role definition last reviewed?
- **Auto-Populate**: From Sheet 2 (Last_Review_Date)
- **Required**: YES

**Q11: Review_Status**
- **Question**: What is the review status?
- **Dropdown**: Current / Overdue-<90-Days / Overdue->90-Days
- **Calculation**:
  - Current: Next_Review_Date ≥ TODAY()
  - Overdue-<90-Days: Next_Review_Date between (TODAY() - 90) and TODAY()
  - Overdue->90-Days: Next_Review_Date < (TODAY() - 90)
- **Compliant**: "Current"

**Q12: Definition_Completeness_Rating**
- **Question**: Overall definition completeness?
- **Dropdown**: Complete / Partial / Incomplete
- **Calculation**:
  - **Complete**: Definition exists, responsibilities complete and specific, authority documented, qualifications documented, accessible, current
  - **Partial**: Definition exists but 1-2 elements incomplete or overdue
  - **Incomplete**: No definition OR multiple critical elements missing
- **Auto-Calculate**: Based on Q3-Q11

**Q13: Gap_Description**
- **Question**: Describe any definition gaps
- **Format**: Free text
- **Examples**:
  - "Role definition overdue for review by 120 days"
  - "Responsibilities documented but vague (e.g., 'manage security')"
  - "No documented authority - unclear who role reports to for escalations"
  - "Role definition exists but not accessible to role holder"
- **Required**: IF Definition_Completeness_Rating ≠ "Complete"

**Q14: Evidence_Reference**
- **Question**: Where is supporting evidence?
- **Format**: Evidence IDs from Sheet 9
- **Example**: "EVD-001 (CISO Job Description), EVD-002 (Role Charter)"

### Sheet 4: RACI_Matrix_Assessment

**Purpose**: Verify RACI matrices exist and are complete for key security processes.

**Assessment Focus**:
- RACI matrix existence for key processes
- RACI completeness (all roles assigned)
- RACI clarity (no ambiguous assignments)
- RACI accuracy (assignments match practice)

**Key Security Processes Requiring RACI** (minimum):
1. Incident Response
2. Access Control (user provisioning/deprovisioning)
3. Change Management
4. Vulnerability Management
5. Risk Assessment
6. Policy Development
7. Security Awareness Training
8. Audit & Compliance
9. Business Continuity
10. Third-Party Security Management

**For each process:**

**Q1: Process_ID**
- **Format**: Pre-filled (PROC-001 through PROC-020)

**Q2: Process_Name**
- **Question**: Name of the security process
- **Format**: Free text
- **Examples**: "Incident Response", "Access Control", "Vulnerability Management"
- **Required**: YES

**Q3: RACI_Matrix_Exists**
- **Question**: Does a RACI matrix exist for this process?
- **Dropdown**: Yes / No
- **Evidence**: RACI matrix document, process documentation
- **Compliant**: "Yes"

**Q4: RACI_Location**
- **Question**: Where is the RACI matrix documented?
- **Format**: Free text - URL or file path
- **Examples**:
  - "ISMS Procedures: /Processes/Incident-Response-RACI.xlsx"
  - "Process Documentation: Confluence /wiki/RACI-Matrices"
- **Required**: IF RACI_Matrix_Exists = "Yes"

**Q5: RACI_Completeness**
- **Question**: Are all relevant roles assigned in RACI?
- **Dropdown**: Complete / Partial / Incomplete
- **Assessment**:
  - Complete: All roles from Sheet 2 (Role_Inventory) assigned where relevant
  - Partial: Some roles assigned, but gaps identified
  - Incomplete: Few or no roles assigned
- **Compliant**: "Complete"

**Q6: RACI_Clarity**
- **Question**: Is RACI clear (no ambiguous assignments)?
- **Dropdown**: Clear / Partially-Clear / Ambiguous
- **Assessment**:
  - Clear: Each process step has ONE "A" (Accountable), clear R/C/I assignments
  - Partially-Clear: Most assignments clear, but some ambiguity
  - Ambiguous: Multiple "A" for same step, unclear who does what
- **RACI Rule**: Each process step MUST have exactly ONE "A" (Accountable)
- **Compliant**: "Clear"

**Q7: RACI_Accuracy**
- **Question**: Does RACI match actual practice?
- **Dropdown**: Accurate / Partially-Accurate / Inaccurate
- **Verification Method**:
  - Interview process participants: "Who actually performs this step?"
  - Compare RACI to recent process executions
  - Check if documented "R" matches actual "R"
- **Compliant**: "Accurate"

**Q8: Last_Update_Date**
- **Question**: When was RACI matrix last updated?
- **Format**: Date (DD.MM.YYYY)
- **Required**: IF RACI_Matrix_Exists = "Yes"

**Q9: RACI_Currency**
- **Question**: Is RACI current (updated within 12 months)?
- **Dropdown**: Current / Overdue
- **Calculation**: If Last_Update_Date < (TODAY() - 365), then "Overdue"
- **Compliant**: "Current"

**Q10: RACI_Compliance_Rating**
- **Question**: Overall RACI compliance for this process?
- **Dropdown**: Compliant / Partial / Non-Compliant
- **Calculation**:
  - **Compliant**: RACI exists, complete, clear, accurate, current
  - **Partial**: RACI exists but 1-2 gaps (e.g., overdue, partially complete)
  - **Non-Compliant**: No RACI OR multiple critical gaps
- **Auto-Calculate**: Based on Q3-Q9

**Q11: Gap_Description**
- **Format**: Free text
- **Examples**:
  - "No RACI matrix for this critical process"
  - "RACI incomplete - Security Analyst role not assigned"
  - "RACI shows 2 Accountable parties for incident escalation - ambiguous"
  - "RACI outdated - documented roles no longer match current org structure"
- **Required**: IF RACI_Compliance_Rating ≠ "Compliant"

**Q12: Evidence_Reference**
- **Format**: Evidence IDs
- **Example**: "EVD-010 (Incident Response RACI), EVD-011 (Interview notes)"

---

**RACI Assessment Tips**:

1. **Verify "A" uniqueness**: Each process step should have EXACTLY ONE "A" (Accountable). If multiple "A", process will have accountability confusion.

2. **Interview participants**: Best way to verify RACI accuracy is to ask people who actually do the work.

3. **Check recent incidents**: Did the documented RACI match what actually happened during last security incident?

4. **Update RACI with org changes**: When roles change (new hires, departures, restructures), RACI must be updated.

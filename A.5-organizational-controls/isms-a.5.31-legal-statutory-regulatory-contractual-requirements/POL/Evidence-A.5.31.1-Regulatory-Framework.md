**Clarification on Compliance Evidence:**

This policy establishes the regulatory compliance framework (identification, assessment, extraction, mapping, monitoring, evidence management). It does NOT establish:

- **Specific regulatory requirements themselves** (documented in ISMS-POL-00 Regulatory Applicability Framework and control-specific policies)
- **Control-specific implementation details** (addressed in individual Annex A control policies like A.8.24, A.5.15-18, etc.)
- **Legal interpretations of regulations** (performed by qualified legal counsel, not policy framework)
- **Technical implementation procedures** (addressed in ISMS-IMP-A.5.31 implementation guides)


The boundary is: POL-A.5.31 framework defines HOW to manage regulatory compliance systematically → POL-00 contains WHAT regulations apply → Individual control policies (A.5.X, A.8.X, etc.) define HOW to meet specific requirements → ISMS-IMP-A.5.31 guides provide operational procedures → Evidence demonstrates compliance with identified requirements.

### Assessment Overview

**Purpose**

This workbook enables systematic planning, scheduling, and tracking of independent reviews of [Organisation]'s information security management system. It ensures reviews are conducted at planned intervals by qualified, independent reviewers as required by ISO 27001:2022 Control A.5.35.

**Scope**

This assessment covers:
- Multi-year review scheduling and planning
- Qualified reviewer identification and registry
- Review scope definition for each engagement
- Review execution tracking
- Findings summary and management reporting
- Evidence collection and retention

**What This Assessment Covers**

| Domain | Assessment Focus |
|--------|-----------------|
| Review Schedule | Multi-year planning and scheduling |
| Reviewer Registry | Qualified reviewer tracking |
| Review Scope | Scope definition per engagement |
| Review Execution | Progress and completion tracking |
| Findings Summary | Review outcomes and key findings |
| Evidence | Audit trail and documentation |

**Control Requirements**

ISO 27001:2022 Control A.5.35 states:
> *"The organisation's approach to managing information security and its implementation (i.e. control objectives, controls, policies, processes and procedures for information security) should be reviewed independently at planned intervals, or when significant changes occur."*

Key requirements:
- Reviews at planned intervals (recommend annual minimum)
- Reviews when significant changes occur
- Reviewer independence from areas under review
- Reviewer competence to conduct reviews
- Management action on identified inadequacies

### Completion Walkthrough

**Step 1: Review_Schedule Sheet**

Establish a multi-year review schedule:

1. **Review_ID** - Unique identifier (format: REV-YYYY-NNN)
2. **Review_Type** - Full ISMS / Partial / Specific Control / Ad-hoc
3. **Planned_Year** - Year the review is scheduled
4. **Planned_Quarter** - Q1, Q2, Q3, or Q4
5. **Planned_Start** - Target start date
6. **Planned_End** - Target completion date
7. **Scope_Summary** - Brief description of review scope
8. **Trigger** - Planned Interval / Significant Change / Regulatory / Other
9. **Status** - Scheduled / In Planning / In Progress / Completed / Cancelled

**Review Frequency Guidance**:

| Review Type | Recommended Frequency |
|-------------|----------------------|
| Full ISMS Review | Annually |
| Policy Review | Every 2 years minimum |
| Control Effectiveness | Annual sampling |
| Targeted/Thematic | As needed based on risk |
| Post-Change Review | Within 3 months of significant change |

**Step 2: Reviewer_Registry Sheet**

Maintain a registry of qualified reviewers:

1. **Reviewer_ID** - Unique identifier
2. **Reviewer_Name** - Full name
3. **Organisation** - Employer (internal/external)
4. **Type** - Internal Auditor / External Auditor / Third-Party Consultant
5. **Qualifications** - Relevant certifications (CISA, ISO 27001 LA, etc.)
6. **Experience_Years** - Years of IS audit experience
7. **Independence_Status** - Independent / Conflict Declared / Not Independent
8. **Approved_Date** - Date reviewer was approved for use
9. **Last_Used** - Date of last engagement
10. **Status** - Active / Inactive / Suspended

**Independence Criteria**:

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Reporting Line | Not in chain of command for area reviewed |
| Operational | No day-to-day responsibility for area |
| Financial | No vested interest in review outcome |
| Personal | No close relationships with area staff |
| Prior Role | Minimum 12 months since working in area |

**Qualification Requirements**:

| Reviewer Type | Minimum Qualifications |
|---------------|------------------------|
| Internal Auditor | Relevant training + 2 years IS experience |
| External Auditor | CISA, ISO 27001 LA, or equivalent + 3 years |
| Third-Party Consultant | Relevant certification + sector experience |

**Step 3: Review_Scope Sheet**

Define scope for each planned review:

1. **Review_ID** - Link to Review_Schedule
2. **Scope_Item** - Specific area/control/process to review
3. **Control_Ref** - ISO 27001 control reference if applicable
4. **Review_Criteria** - Standards and requirements to assess against
5. **Methodology** - Document review / Interview / Testing / Observation
6. **Sample_Approach** - Full / Statistical / Judgmental
7. **Key_Contacts** - Personnel to interview
8. **Documentation_Required** - Evidence to request
9. **Priority** - High / Medium / Low

**Scope Definition Tips**:

- Include all ISMS components over a 3-year cycle
- Focus on high-risk areas more frequently
- Consider regulatory requirements
- Include lessons learned from previous reviews
- Account for recent changes to include in scope

**Step 4: Review_Execution Sheet**

Track review progress:

1. **Review_ID** - Link to Review_Schedule
2. **Phase** - Planning / Fieldwork / Reporting / Closure
3. **Activity** - Specific review activity
4. **Assigned_Reviewer** - Link to Reviewer_Registry
5. **Planned_Start** - Target start date
6. **Actual_Start** - When activity began
7. **Planned_End** - Target completion
8. **Actual_End** - When completed
9. **Status** - Not Started / In Progress / Complete / Delayed
10. **Notes** - Progress comments

**Review Phases**:

| Phase | Activities |
|-------|------------|
| Planning | Scope finalisation, resource allocation, logistics |
| Fieldwork | Document review, interviews, testing, observation |
| Reporting | Draft report, management response, final report |
| Closure | Report distribution, action tracking, evidence filing |

**Step 5: Findings_Summary Sheet**

Record review outcomes:

1. **Review_ID** - Link to Review_Schedule
2. **Total_Findings** - Number of findings
3. **Critical** - Count of critical findings
4. **High** - Count of high findings
5. **Medium** - Count of medium findings
6. **Low** - Count of low findings
7. **Observations** - Count of improvement observations
8. **Overall_Opinion** - Effective / Partially Effective / Ineffective
9. **Key_Strengths** - Summary of positive findings
10. **Key_Weaknesses** - Summary of concerns
11. **Report_Date** - Date report issued
12. **Report_Ref** - Reference to full report

**Step 6: Evidence_Register Sheet**

Link supporting evidence:

1. **Evidence_ID** - Unique identifier
2. **Review_ID** - Link to Review_Schedule
3. **Evidence_Type** - Review Plan / Scope Document / Working Papers / Report / Sign-off
4. **Description** - Brief description
5. **Storage_Location** - File path or system reference
6. **Retention_Until** - Retention end date (minimum 7 years)
7. **Classification** - Document classification level

**Step 7: Approval_SignOff Sheet**

Obtain authorisation for the review programme:

1. **Approval_Type** - Annual Schedule / Individual Review / Reviewer Approval
2. **Approved_By** - Role of approver
3. **Name** - Approver name
4. **Signature** - Signature field
5. **Date** - Approval date
6. **Comments** - Any conditions or notes

### Common Pitfalls

❌ **MISTAKE**: Scheduling reviews without confirmed reviewer availability
✅ **CORRECT**: Confirm reviewer availability before finalising schedule

❌ **MISTAKE**: Using reviewers without verified independence
✅ **CORRECT**: Document independence assessment for each engagement

❌ **MISTAKE**: Vague scope definitions that lead to scope creep
✅ **CORRECT**: Define specific controls, processes, and boundaries

❌ **MISTAKE**: Not tracking review progress against plan
✅ **CORRECT**: Update execution tracking weekly during active reviews

❌ **MISTAKE**: Relying on verbal feedback without documentation
✅ **CORRECT**: All findings documented in formal review report

❌ **MISTAKE**: No follow-up on previous review findings
✅ **CORRECT**: Include prior finding status in next review scope

❌ **MISTAKE**: Scheduling reviews too close to business peak periods
✅ **CORRECT**: Consider business calendar when planning

❌ **MISTAKE**: Single point of failure with one qualified reviewer
✅ **CORRECT**: Maintain multiple approved reviewers

### Quality Checklist

Before submitting:

- [ ] Multi-year review schedule approved by CISO
- [ ] At least 2 qualified reviewers registered
- [ ] Independence verified for all active reviewers
- [ ] Review scope documented before each review starts
- [ ] Execution progress updated during active reviews
- [ ] All completed reviews have findings summary
- [ ] Evidence properly filed and referenced
- [ ] Approval sign-offs obtained

### Review & Approval

**Review Workflow**:

1. Information Security Manager drafts annual review schedule
2. CISO reviews and approves schedule
3. For each review: scope defined, reviewer assigned, independence verified
4. Review executed per plan
5. Findings reported to management
6. Actions tracked to closure

**Approval Requirements**:

| Item | Approver | Timing |
|------|----------|--------|
| Annual Schedule | CISO | Before year start |
| Reviewer Approval | CISO | Before first engagement |
| Review Scope | Information Security Manager | Before review starts |
| Final Report | CISO | Within 10 days of draft |

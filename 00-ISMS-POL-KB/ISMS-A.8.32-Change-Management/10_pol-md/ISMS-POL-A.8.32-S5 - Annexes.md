## 5.1 Purpose

This document serves as index for policy annexes providing supporting materials, templates, and reference information for change management policy implementation.

---

## 5.2 Annex Overview

The change management policy framework includes four annexes:

| Annex | Title | Purpose | Pages |
|-------|-------|---------|-------|
| **S5.A** | Change Management Capability Standards | Technical requirements for change management systems | ~10 |
| **S5.B** | Change Request Form Template | Standard change request template | ~5 |
| **S5.C** | Risk Assessment Matrix | Change risk classification methodology | ~6 |
| **S5.D** | Quick Reference Guide | One-page practitioner guide | ~4 |

---

## 5.3 Annex Descriptions

### 5.3.1 Annex A - Capability Standards (S5.A)

**Purpose:** Define minimum capabilities required from change management systems (ITSM tools, ticketing systems, workflow platforms).

**Content:**
- Change request management capabilities
- Workflow automation requirements
- Approval workflow capabilities
- Integration requirements
- Reporting and analytics capabilities
- Audit trail requirements

**Audience:** IT Operations, ITSM tool administrators, procurement teams

**Use:** Evaluate existing systems, select new tools, configure implementations

---

### 5.3.2 Annex B - Change Request Form Template (S5.B)

**Purpose:** Provide standard template for change requests ensuring consistent information capture.

**Content:**
- Required fields and descriptions
- Field validation rules
- Help text for requesters
- Example completed form

**Audience:** Change requesters, change management system administrators

**Use:** Configure ITSM tool change request forms, train requesters, ensure completeness

---

### 5.3.3 Annex C - Risk Assessment Matrix (S5.C)

**Purpose:** Provide detailed methodology for assessing change risk based on impact and likelihood.

**Content:**
- Impact level definitions and examples
- Likelihood level definitions and examples
- Risk matrix (Impact × Likelihood)
- Approval authority mapping by risk level
- Risk mitigation strategies

**Audience:** Change requesters, CAB members, change managers

**Use:** Consistent risk assessment across all changes, determine approval requirements

---

### 5.3.4 Annex D - Quick Reference Guide (S5.D)

**Purpose:** One-page summary for practitioners covering most common change management scenarios.

**Content:**
- Change type decision tree
- Quick approval authority reference
- Emergency change checklist
- Key contacts and escalation paths

**Audience:** All IT staff, change implementers, first-time requesters

**Use:** Day-to-day reference, training aid, desk reference

## 5.4 Annex Maintenance

**Review Frequency:**
- Annexes reviewed annually (aligned with master policy review)
- Annexes may be updated independently without full policy review
- Minor updates (examples, clarifications) do not require version change
- Major updates (structural changes, new requirements) trigger version update

**Update Authority:**
- Annex A, C: CISO + Change Manager approval
- Annex B: Change Manager approval
- Annex D: Change Manager approval

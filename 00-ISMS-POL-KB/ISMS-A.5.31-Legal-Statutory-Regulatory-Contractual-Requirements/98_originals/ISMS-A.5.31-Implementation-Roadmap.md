## KEY PRINCIPLES FOR ALL CONTENT

### Universal Requirements:
1. **Completely Generic & Industry-Agnostic**
   - Use "[Organization]" throughout
   - No specific regulations mentioned (use "Regulation X", "Data Protection Law", etc. in examples)
   - Framework works for ANY regulatory landscape

2. **Systems Engineering Approach**
   - Systematic processes, not ad-hoc
   - Repeatable by different people
   - Evidence-based decisions
   - Traceable from regulation → requirement → control → evidence

3. **Anti-Cargo-Cult Compliance**
   - Never just "we comply with applicable laws"
   - Always show HOW compliance is achieved
   - Emphasis on methodology and process
   - Audit-ready evidence framework

4. **Relationship to ISMS-POL-00**
   - POL-00 is the regulatory register (the "what")
   - A.5.31 is the framework (the "how")
   - A.5.31 governs how POL-00 is maintained
   - Clear governance relationship

### Writing Style:
- **Policy Sections**: Strategic, business-focused, defines WHAT and WHY
- **Implementation Sections**: Tactical, operational, step-by-step HOW
- **Professional but accessible**: Not overly formal
- **Systematic language**: "Process", "framework", "methodology"
- **Traceability emphasis**: Always show the thread

### Examples:
- **Always generic**: Use placeholder regulation names
- **Multiple scenarios**: Show different regulation types
- **Complete worked examples**: Show entire process, not just snippets
- **Realistic but anonymous**: Plausible scenarios without real entities
